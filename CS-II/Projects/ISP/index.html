2022 ISP Project Idea:

Our ISP will be a website that hosts Python/Swift code inside that will be a questionare for those looking for advice about mental health issues and dealing with them. The code will be interactive with collecting data from the user while keeping them anonymous with the session. At the end, it will give a specific answer best fit for their problems based on the questions. It will also be very polished with animations and have many options for questions to enlighten the user. We might also design a mascot for the website.
